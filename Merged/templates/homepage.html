Fake Review Detection System

Our Fake Review Detection System Web App is designed to detect and flag suspicious reviews across multiple languages, including Hindi and English. Using state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms, our system analyzes text content, identifying patterns and linguistic markers indicative of potential fake or manipulated reviews. The app offers a user-friendly interface where users can input reviews in either Hindi or English, and receive instant feedback on their authenticity. By harnessing the power of natural language processing, our system contributes to a fairer and more trustworthy online review ecosystem, empowering consumers to make informed decisions.
